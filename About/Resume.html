Interactive Resumes

I was interested in how my resumes and skill sets have changed over the years so I decided to conduct a little experiment. The following resumes were parsed and tokenized using Intersystems Cache Mumps. The nodes represend entities and the edges represent verbs linking those entities. Groupings of related concepts are highlighted in seperate colors. The graphs are presented in embedded iframes and contain zoom and pan capabilities.
